## 1. Business problem statements
A leading retail company wants to better understand its customersâ€™ shopping behavior in order to improve sales, customer satisfaction, and long-term loyalty. The management team has noticed changes in purchasing patterns across demographics, product categories, and sales channels (online vs. offline). They are particularly interested in uncovering which factors, such as discounts, reviews, seasons, or payment preferences, drive consumer decisions and repeat purchases.

## 2. Description
Customer shopping behaviour dashboard is build in Power BI to analyzed customer shopping behavior using transactional sales data to understand purchase patterns, customer segments, buying frequency, and product preferences. Generated actionable insights to improve customer targeting, retention strategies, and personalized marketing decisions.

## 3. Goal of this dashboard
To analyze customer purchasing patterns and behavior using sales transaction data in order to identify key customer segments, understand buying frequency and product preferences, and provide actionable insights that help improve customer retention, personalized marketing, and overall business revenue.

## 5. The key visuals
At the top, prominent KPI cards display the total number of customers (3.9K), total purchase amount (233K), average purchase value ($59.76), and average review rating (3.75), giving an instant overview of overall performance. A left-side filter panel allows users to interact with the data using subscription status, gender, product category, and shipping type, making the dashboard dynamic and suitable for deeper analysis across different customer segments.
The visual section highlights customer behavior and sales trends using intuitive charts. A donut chart shows that the majority of customers are non-subscribers (73%) compared to subscribers (27%). Bar charts reveal that clothing leads both in revenue and sales volume, followed by accessories, footwear, and outerwear. Additional horizontal bar charts analyze revenue and sales by age group, where young adults emerge as the strongest contributors, with middle-aged, adult, and senior groups following. Overall, the dashboard uses a consistent blue theme, clear spacing, and easy-to-read visuals, making it effective for data-driven decision-making and suitable for presentation in an analytics or portfolio project.
